Welcome to Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, known as the Pearl of the Indian Ocean, is famous for its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture.

Key Highlights of Sri Lanka

  • Capital: Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte (Administrative), Colombo (Commercial)
  • Languages: Sinhala, Tamil, English
  • Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR)
  • Famous Landmarks: Sigiriya, Temple of the Tooth, Galle Fort, Adam's Peak
  • Wildlife: Elephants, Leopards, Blue Whales
  • Traditional Cuisine: Rice & Curry, Hoppers, Kottu Roti, String Hoppers
